Welcome to Castleknock veterinary clinic

Hey there! We're a friendly vet clinic that's all about taking great care of your furry friends. You can find us on the main street of Castleknock in Dublin 15. Our awesome team is here to help your pets feel their best with top-notch medical, surgical, and dental care. But we're not just about fixing things when they're broken, we're all about making sure your pets stay happy and healthy throughout their lives. We're big on responsible pet ownership, preventive healthcare, and keeping a close eye on your pet's well-being. At Vets & Pets Veterinary Clinic, we're all about giving you and your pets the best possible service. So, don't be shy: reach out to us today and let's chat about how we can keep your pets feeling their absolute best

How can we help

  • Vaccines: At our clinic, we provide a range of vaccines to protect your pets against diseases and illnesses.
  • Parasites: In our clinic, we provide advice and assistance on various Parasite Control Programs, which cover issues like Fleas & Ticks, Heartworm, Tapeworm, Hookworm, Roundworm, and Whipworm.
  • Full Diagnostics: We take great pride in providing a comprehensive diagnostic laboratory right here at our clinic. With our in-house laboratory machines, we can conduct full blood cell counts, comprehensive chemistry panels (including tests for liver, kidney, and pancreatic enzyme function), check thyroid levels, monitor glucose, and assess electrolytes
  • General Surgery: We're thrilled to offer a wide range of general surgery services, all carried out under general anesthesia, and we make sure to provide proper post-anesthetic pain management.
  • Anesthesia Services: Every patient is given injectable pain medication during the procedure, ensuring that they wake up feeling comfortable and at ease.
  • Dentistry: We're delighted to provide a range of dentistry services, which encompass prophylaxis (teeth cleaning), polishing, extractions, and a comprehensive oral examination.
  • Euthanasia: Our compassionate staff is here to support you during the tough decision of saying goodbye to your beloved pet through euthanasia.
  • Ear Checks: In our clinic, we provide detailed ear exams to investigate symptoms like head shaking, scratching, pawing at the ears, rubbing ears on surfaces, whining, and unusual odors.
  • Allergy & Itchy Skin Relief: When it comes to relieving allergies and itchy skin, we're here to help. We can evaluate your pet's skin, talk about their symptoms, and work together to figure out the right medication or diet plan to get your pet back to feeling great. Allergies can be well-managed, so don't worry
